Experiment on factors affecting machinability of MDN250 in wire-cut EDM

Guided by a Scientist
Feb 2020 - Mar 2020

We have investigated the effect of input parameters such as pulse-on time, feed rate, servo control reference mean voltage on machining performance of the wire electrical discharge machining through the variation in output parameters namely, surface roughness, material removal rate, average kerf width, etc. Material made of maraging steel 250 is used for the experiment. Based on the experimental values, a regression model was created. The Taguchi method is being used to analyze the experiment. Response optimization was done to get optimum values for specific conditions.
